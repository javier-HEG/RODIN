Hello and welcome on Portaneo Enterprise Portal !

This application will help you access the information, the people, and the tools you need, on a single interface.
To begin with Portaneo, you can fill in your profile, and create you first page customized to your needs.
Then, several menus will give you access to the publication, communication and network tools :
  • My profile allows you to update your profile information
  • Add content to add widgets in my pages
  • My notebook menu give you access to your notebook (publication space), and your network notebooks
  • My network allows you to create your people network and working groups, and to manage them
  • My groups to manage my groups and add information to linked notebooks
  • Other options to manage the pages options, other applications, ...
